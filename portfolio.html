Global Business Support Materials Website Enhancements

Poster for Reverse Career Fair

This is a poster made for the Amway Intern Reverse Career Fair. I discussed the project that I was working on: Global Business Support Materials Website Enhancements (BSM). This web application allows Amway Business Owners (ABO) to store their materials in a central database. This allows for easy access for each ABO and the Global BSM department at Amway. My role was the software tester, making sure the functionality of the website was intact and fit the requirements set by the Business Systems Analyst. I do this by writing test cases, executing them, and then reporting any bugs to the developer.
