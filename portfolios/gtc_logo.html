Crafting a brand logo for an emerging startup

Good Tree Capital is a lending platform that empowers small Cannabis businesses to receive loans through private investors. I was brought on to create a new logo for the company

The previous logo for the company

Requirements

In order to focus my efforts in a certain directions, I worked with the CEO and Marketing Director of the company to agree on some requirements for the new logo. We agreed that the logo would represent these themes:

  • Technology - As our proprietary technology is integral to our business model, we want to be seen as a fintech company.
  • Growth - We want our logo to communicate a sense of upward momentum and growth.
  • Green We want to form an identity and visual style around a single color. We chose the color green becuase it often represents wealth, growth, and vitality.
  • Tree - Our company is called Good Tree Capital. Tree. We want to reference this in the logo.

Inspiration

I first sourced inspiration online that related to the requirements we had specified.

Creative inspiration for the new logo

Explorations

Having the requirements laid out and much inspiration to draw from, I began creating many different logos. My goal was to create many variations for the team to look over and provide feedback.

My logo explorations. I was initially drawn to the hexagon shape because it made the tree look more modern.

Final designs

After getting feedback from the rest of the team, we were able to align on one of the exploration. With the logo shape decided, I began building out how the logo would manifest in other situations.
